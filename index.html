Frequently asked questions

Good to know

Who can apply?

Any Kenyan citizen aged 18 or older, with a valid National ID, an active mobile number, and a regular source of income.

How long does the application take?

Most applicants finish in under five minutes. The eligibility assessment itself takes only a few seconds.

Is my information secure?

In this demo, your details are stored only on your own device and are never transmitted anywhere.

What documents are required?

Just your National ID number and basic details about your employment and income — no uploads needed.

Are there any fees to apply or receive an offer?

No. Checking your eligibility and receiving an offer is completely free. We will never ask you to pay a fee to "unlock," "release," or "clear" a loan.
